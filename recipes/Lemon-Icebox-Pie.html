Lemon Icebox Pie

Description

A family favorite when a no-bake, fast pie is needed. (And family members too small to use the oven can make dessert!) Very pretty when garnished with whipped cream and mint leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
  2. Add condensed milk, lemon juice, and lemon rind.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into crust.
  5. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
  6. Garnish with whipped cream and mint leaves if desired.
